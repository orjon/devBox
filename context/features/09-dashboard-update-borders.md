# Update dashboard boarder colours & formatting

## Overview

For item cards (Recent and Pinned) use the same coloured left side border as per collections, and for those same items, remove the coloured border from the rest of the sides. 

So in the end, collections have ticker band of colour on left, and  same colour on the other sides of the border. Recent and Pinned items will only have a coloured band on the left side, the top,right and bottom will just have a grey border like the summary cards at the top of the dash board.

## Requirements

Make the styling changes required to implement the above. No functional changes.
